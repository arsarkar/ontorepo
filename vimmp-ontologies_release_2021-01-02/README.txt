=============================================
VIMMP ontology release dated 2nd January 2021
=============================================

This work was funded from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research
and innovation programme under grant agreement no. 760907, Virtual
Materials Marketplace (VIMMP). It is released by the VIMMP project
consortium under the terms and conditions of LGPL version 3.

=======
Content
=======

Introduction and overview in the directory:
   doc/

Separate ontology files in the directories:
   alignment/
   macro/
   mmto/
   osmo/
   otras/
   vico/
   viso/
   vivo/
   vov/

Combined ontology file in the directory:
   combined/

Example scenario descriptions in the directories:
   scenario/eos-parameterization/
   scenario/hpc-job/
   scenario/molecular-models/
   scenario/self-descriptions/
   scenario/software/
   scenario/summer-school/
   scenario/training/
